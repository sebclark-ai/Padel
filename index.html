Common Questions
FAQ
Do I need to be a member to book a court?
No — anyone can book a court through Playtomic. No account required for one-off bookings. Membership gives you discounted rates and priority booking.
I've never played padel — is it suitable for beginners?
Absolutely. Padel combines elements of tennis and squash — most people are hitting rallies within their first 20 minutes. We offer coaching sessions for all levels too.
Can I hire equipment?
Yes — rackets and balls are available to hire on-site when you arrive. Just show up in trainers and comfortable athletic wear.
Is there parking?
Yes — we have a large dedicated free car park. The club is at Unit 4C, Simmonds Road, Wincheap Estate, Canterbury, CT1 3RA.
What are your opening hours?
We're open every day, 6am to 11pm — 365 days a year. Off-peak slots before midday and after 10pm are available at lower rates.
FAQ kills the last objections before they close the tab. "Never played" and "do I need a membership" are the two highest-friction questions for cold traffic from Meta ads. Answer them here so they don't go Google it and not come back.

Common Questions
FAQ
Can I cancel my membership at any time?
Yes — no lock-in contracts. Cancel with 30 days' notice at any time. No hidden fees.
Is membership worth it if I only play once a week?
Work From Home (£25/mo) breaks even at 2–3 off-peak weekday sessions. Green (£50/mo) breaks even around 4–5 sessions per month at standard rates. Run the numbers above.
Can I bring guests if I'm a member?
All tiers use standard court rates for guests — book extra slots through Playtomic and your discount applies.
What if I want to upgrade my membership tier?
Easy — contact us at info@brakepointpadel.com and we'll upgrade your plan from the next billing cycle.
